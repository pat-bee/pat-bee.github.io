Neurological & cognitive effects

Super Lemon Haze: terpinolene-forward

Often described as clear + energized. Terpinolene is frequently discussed as supporting mental stimulation with some calming balance, while caryophyllene is commonly associated with stress-modulating effects.

Moonshine Haze: terpinolene + pinene duo

This profile adds pinene into the mix, which is often discussed in relation to alertness and memory-like clarity. Combined with terpinolene, users commonly describe a creative + socially fluid experience.

Reported vibe: talky, idea-sparkly, collaborative energy.
