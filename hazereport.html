Neurological & Cognitive Effects

Super Lemon Haze: Terpinolene-Dominant Profile

Super Lemon Haze's primary terpene, terpinolene, demonstrates sustained cognitive clarity through antioxidant neuroprotection1. Research indicates terpinolene provides mental stimulation while maintaining relaxation, making it ideal for focus-intensive tasks without overstimulation2. The dominance of caryophyllene activates CB2 receptors, reducing stress-related cognitive interference3. Users report enhanced focus, energy, and uplift‚Äîparticularly suited for daytime use.

Moonshine Haze: Balanced Terpene Synergy

Moonshine Haze combines terpinolene with pinene, creating multi-pathway cognitive enhancement4. While terpinolene provides mental clarity, pinene's inhibition of acetylcholinesterase improves memory retention and alertness5. This dual mechanism supports creative thinking and sustained attention. Caryophyllene's anti-inflammatory effects further reduce neuroinflammation that impairs cognitive performance6. Users report creativity, focus, and euphoria with social ease.

Key Neurological Differences

Cognitive Focus Architecture Super Lemon Haze emphasizes sustained attentional clarity through terpinolene's antioxidant neuroprotection; Moonshine Haze adds memory-specific enhancement via pinene's acetylcholinesterase inhibition7.
