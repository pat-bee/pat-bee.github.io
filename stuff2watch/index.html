Widow’s Bay

Click to read Wikipedia entry

2026 • 14+ • 41 minutes per episode

Widow’s Bay is a coastal mystery series about a mayor trying to revive a supposedly cursed New England island town, only to discover that the local folklore may be less quaint than he hoped. The show mixes eerie atmosphere, small-town politics, and darkly comic character work in a way that makes it feel more specific than a generic supernatural thriller. Its best part is the tension between civic boosterism and genuine dread, which gives the premise both humor and stakes. Its weakest part is that the tonal blend may feel a little uneven if you want either straight horror or a cleaner, more conventional mystery.
